Your Daily Practice

A simple 3-step
framework.

Five minutes each day. Three honest questions. No pressure, no perfection — just you, showing up for yourself.

  • 1

    How do I feel today?

    Check in with your emotions. Name it without judgment.

  • 2

    What do I want this day to bring?

    Set an intention. What would make today feel meaningful?

  • 3

    Three things I'm grateful for.

    Gratitude shifts everything. Big or small — name them.
